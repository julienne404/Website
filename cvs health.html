CVS Health: Green Initiative

Collaborators: Amy T. and Leona D.

While co-running the Claremont Colleges' Design for America chapter during the fall 2020 semester, Leona and I participated in the National Challenge with our appointed project team lead Amy. The problem we were presented with was a lack of engagement with sustainable products under the CVS Health umbrella. Coordinating with VPs of Product Management and mentors at DFA National headquarters, we delved into empathy and needfinding stages of the design process. More about this can be found in our jamboard

How might we utilize effective branding strategies to maximize reach of “green” products across demographics?
